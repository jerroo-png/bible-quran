About God and His People

Quran 25:63 The servants of the Most Merciful are those who walk on the earth humbly, and when the ignorant address them, they say, "Peace."
1 Peter 2:9 But you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, God’s special possession...
Quran 2:152 Remember Me, and I will remember you.
Jeremiah 29:11 For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord...
Quran 39:53 Do not despair of the mercy of Allah.
Psalm 23:1 The Lord is my shepherd, I lack nothing.

Both speak of God choosing and caring for His people with mercy, guidance, and closeness.
